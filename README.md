🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis

This project analyzes Diwali sales data using Python to understand customer purchasing behavior and identify key business insights. The analysis helps businesses improve marketing strategies and increase sales during festive seasons.

📊 Objective

The main objective of this project is to explore sales data and discover patterns related to customer demographics, product categories, and purchasing power during the Diwali festival.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Python

Pandas

NumPy

Matplotlib

Seaborn

Jupyter Notebook

🔍 Key Analysis Performed

-Data cleaning and preprocessing of raw sales data

-Analysis of gender-wise purchasing behavior

-Identification of age groups contributing the most to sales

-State-wise analysis of customer orders

-Analysis of top-selling product categories

-Identification of high spending customer segments

📈 Key Insights

Most purchases were made by married women aged 26–35 years.

Customers from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka contributed the highest number of orders.

Food, Clothing, and Electronics were among the most purchased product categories.

🎯 Conclusion

The analysis shows that businesses should focus their marketing strategies on married women in the 26–35 age group, especially in high-purchasing states, to maximize sales during festive seasons like Diwali.
